# Tapas Tracker

[**🚀 Visit the Live App**](https://tapas-tracker.app/)

**Tapas Tracker** is an open-source tool designed for individual development and self-reflection.
*Helping you check your progress, track your habits, plan and adapt your steps, and stay focused on your goals.*

[![License: AGPL-3.0](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-AGPL_3.0-blue.svg)](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/agpl-3.0)
[![Next.js](https://img.shields.io/badge/Next.js-15.x-black)](https://nextjs.org/)

## 🚀 Features

* **Track your individual strivings:** Log your daily actions and monitor your personal growth.
* **Define your goals:** Set custom activity schedules, define clear targets, and plan your commitments.
* **Build consistency:** Log your completed activities and track your progress over time.
* **Shared Strivings with Multilingual Support:** Join shared activities with others (you can provide multiple translations).
* **Reflect and adapt:** Document your results, reflect on your journey, and refine your goals whenever you need to restart or adjust.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

* **Framework:** [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router)
* **Styling:** [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
* **Language:** JavaScript / TypeScript

## 📦 Getting Started

Ensure you have [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/) (version 18+) installed on your system.

1. **Clone the repository:**
   ```bash
   git clone [https://github.com/ReimundR/tapas-tracker.git](https://github.com/ReimundR/tapas-tracker.git)
   cd tapas-tracker```

2. **Install dependencies:**
    ```npm install
    # or
    yarn install
    # or
    pnpm install```

3. **Run the development server:**
    ```npm run dev```

4. **View the app:**
    Open http://localhost:3000 in your browser.
